Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy explains what Elite Indian Investors ("EII", "we", "us") collects when you use our Telegram-based stock research and scoring service, and how that information is used.

1. Information we collect

  • Telegram account details — your Telegram user ID and display name, provided automatically by Telegram when you message our bot.
  • Subscription and billing status — your subscription ID, plan status (active, cancelled, etc.), and billing period dates.
  • Payment information — payments are processed directly by Razorpay. We do not receive or store your card, UPI, or bank details — only the subscription status Razorpay reports back to us.

2. How we use this information

  • To verify that you have an active subscription before sending the alert digest.
  • To deliver your stock scoring digest to you via Telegram.
  • To respond if you contact us for support.

3. Third parties we share data with

We use two processors to run this service:

  • Razorpay — handles all payment processing and billing. Razorpay's own privacy policy governs how it handles your payment data.
  • Telegram — the messaging platform used to deliver your scoring digest. Telegram's own privacy policy governs how it handles your account data.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your information with any other third party.

4. Data retention

We keep subscription records for as long as your subscription is active, and for a reasonable period afterward for billing and support history. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Security

Subscription records are stored in access-controlled systems. Payment credentials are never handled or stored by us directly — that responsibility sits entirely with Razorpay.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you at any time by contacting us — see the Contact Us page. Deleting your data will end your subscription and digest delivery.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the details on our Contact Us page.
