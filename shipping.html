Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Elite Indian Investors ("EII", "we", "us") provides a digital subscription service. There is no physical product and nothing is shipped. This page explains how delivery works instead.

1. Nature of delivery

Your subscription is delivered entirely online, via access to a private Telegram group. There is no courier, postal shipment, or physical item involved at any stage.

2. Delivery timeline

  • Access to the private Telegram group is granted automatically once Razorpay confirms your payment.
  • In the normal case, this happens within a few minutes of a successful payment.
  • If access is not granted within 30 minutes of a successful payment, contact us via the Contact Us page and we will resolve it.

3. Delivery area

Since delivery is digital, the service is available anywhere Telegram itself is accessible — there are no geographic shipping restrictions.

4. Failed or delayed delivery

If your payment succeeds but group access fails to activate, this is treated as a service issue, not a shipping issue. Reach us via the Contact Us page and we will grant access manually or investigate the underlying failure.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
