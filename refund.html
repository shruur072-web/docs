Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Elite Indian Investors runs one plan: a ₹499/month recurring subscription billed through Razorpay. This page explains how cancellation and refunds work.

1. Cancelling your subscription

  • You can cancel at any time — there is no minimum term and no lock-in period.
  • Cancelling stops future billing. You keep access to the scoring digest until the end of the billing cycle you've already paid for.
  • To cancel, contact us using the details on the Contact Us page, or cancel directly through Razorpay if you manage your subscription from your Razorpay account.

2. Refunds

  • Payments already processed for the current billing cycle are non-refundable once the cycle has started.
  • We do not offer partial or prorated refunds for unused days within a paid cycle.
  • If you are charged in error — for example, a duplicate charge, or a charge after you had already cancelled — contact us within 7 days of the charge and we will investigate and refund the erroneous amount.

3. Failed or disputed payments

If a payment fails, Razorpay may retry it automatically as part of its standard billing process. If you believe a charge was made incorrectly, raise it with us first via the Contact Us page before initiating a dispute with your bank or card issuer, so we can resolve it directly and quickly.

4. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
